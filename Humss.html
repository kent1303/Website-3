HUMSS Strand

The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand is designed for students who are interested in pursuing careers in social sciences, communication, liberal arts, and education. This strand equips students with the skills and knowledge necessary for understanding human behavior, society, and culture.

Why Choose HUMSS?

  • Learn critical thinking, research, and communication skills.
  • Develop a deeper understanding of human behavior and social structures.
  • Prepare for college courses in education, journalism, psychology, and more.

Subjects in HUMSS

Some of the core subjects include:

  • Creative Writing
  • Introduction to World Religions and Belief Systems
  • Philosophy
  • Social Science Research
  • Political Science

Career Paths

Graduates of the HUMSS strand can pursue careers in:

  • Teaching
  • Journalism
  • Psychology
  • Social Work
  • Public Relations
  • Law

Discover your passion for understanding humanity and society through the HUMSS strand!
