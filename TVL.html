Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Strand

The Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) strand is designed for senior high school students who are inclined to practical and technical skills. This strand offers specialized courses that prepare students for immediate employment, entrepreneurship, or further education in their chosen fields.

TVL provides hands-on training in areas such as:

  • Information and Communications Technology (ICT)
  • Industrial Arts
  • Agri-Fishery Arts
  • Home Economics

By completing the TVL strand, students gain National Certificates (NCs) issued by the Technical Education and Skills Development Authority (TESDA), which serve as proof of competency in their chosen skills.

This strand is ideal for students who want to:

  • Enter the workforce immediately after senior high school.
  • Start their own business.
  • Pursue college degrees related to technical and vocational fields.

Explore endless possibilities with the TVL strand and build a solid foundation for a brighter future!
