ROSALES NATIONAL HIGH SCHOOL

Rosales National High School Senior High School (SHS) is committed to providing quality education that prepares students for college, careers, and life. Our school offers a supportive and nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, leadership, and innovation.

At Rosales National High School SHS, we are proud to offer a range of academic tracks and specialized programs designed to meet the needs of every student. With experienced faculty and modern facilities, we ensure that our students receive the best education possible.
