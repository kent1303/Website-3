General Academic Strand (GAS)

The General Academic Strand (GAS) is designed for senior high school students who prefer a flexible approach to their studies. It provides a wide range of subjects, allowing students to explore various fields such as business, humanities, social sciences, and more.

GAS is perfect for students who are still deciding on their future career paths, offering a balanced curriculum that helps them discover their interests and potential. With GAS, you can acquire skills and knowledge that prepare you for diverse opportunities in higher education or the workplace.
